These NFL Teams Have Never Won the Super Bowl

SAFF Cup 2023: India and Pakistan to renew football rivalry; clubbed in same group

India and Pakistan are set to renew their football rivalry after nearly five years as the two neighbouring countries have been clubbed in the same group in the SAFF Cup to be held in Bengaluru from June 21 to July 4. The draw of the 14th edition of the South Asian Football Federation Cup was held here on Wednesday, with defending champion India, along with Pakistan, Kuwait and Nepal, being clubbed in Group A. Lebanon, Maldives, Bangladesh, and Bhutan are in Group B.
